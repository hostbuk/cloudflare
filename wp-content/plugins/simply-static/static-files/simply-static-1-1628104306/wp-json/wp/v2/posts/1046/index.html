Welding is one of the most common processes which is used for the amalgamation of metals by applying a different set of techniques. To make it easy, welding is a process in which we use a high heat source, filler metal, the joining metals and any kind of protective shield from the air. While having protection from the air the joining metals are heated until they melt, then put on the filler metal in the heated region to get a unique piece of metal.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

It is not compulsory to have a filler metal for the completion of the process.It can be done without filler metal. The purpose of filler metal is just to create a strong bond between joining metals. Welding process can be done at different locations, such as in outdoor setting or indoor sites e.g. factories and construction sites. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

Welding should be done very consciously if done wrongly, it can lead to many damages. So it\u2019s very necessary to have proper knowledge of welding techniques and skill for performing weld operations. There are various techniques of welding such as gas metal arc welding (GMAW) and shielded metal arc welding (SMAW), spot welding and tack welding. In this article, we will discuss tack welding and its proper usage.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
